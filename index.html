Witchlight

Witchlight is a new tabletop skirmish game set in an ancient world, amidst the birth of human civilisation a powerful caste of witches seeks power in a decayed magical realm.

Witchlight is currently in development and there's lots of work to do. Art, sculpting miniatures, painting, lore, rules, this very website... Rather than keep it all a secret you can follow the progress here by taking a look in the News Section or by following Callie on Mastodon or Instagram
